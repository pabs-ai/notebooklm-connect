# Privacy Policy for NotebookLM Connect

**Last Updated:** December 21, 2024

## Overview

NotebookLM Connect ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

**We collect ZERO personal information.**

The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

### Local Storage Only

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage API to save:
- Number of pages you've sent (count only)
- Title and URL of your last sent page
- Timestamp of when you last used the extension

This data:
- ✅ Stays on your device only
- ✅ Never leaves your browser
- ✅ Is not transmitted to any server
- ✅ Is not accessible to us or any third party
- ✅ Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Content you send to NotebookLM
- ❌ URLs you visit
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Cookies or identifiers
- ❌ IP addresses
- ❌ Any personally identifiable information

## How the Extension Works

1. **When you right-click "Send to NotebookLM":**
   - The Extension extracts content from the current page
   - Content is processed locally in your browser
   - Formatted content is prepared for NotebookLM
   - Nothing is sent to our servers (we don't have any!)

2. **When NotebookLM opens:**
   - Content is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage
   - You manually copy and paste it into NotebookLM
   - We never see or access this content

## Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with:
- **Google NotebookLM** - We open their website in a new tab
- Your use of NotebookLM is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
- We do not share any data with Google or NotebookLM
- We are not affiliated with Google

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests these Chrome permissions:

### activeTab
- **Why:** To read content from the current webpage when you click "Send"
- **What we do:** Extract article text, title, and URL
- **What we don't do:** Access tabs you're not actively using

### contextMenus
- **Why:** To add "Send to NotebookLM" to right-click menu
- **What we do:** Create the menu option
- **What we don't do:** Track when or where you right-click

### scripting
- **Why:** To inject content extraction code into pages
- **What we do:** Extract article content intelligently
- **What we don't do:** Modify pages or inject ads/trackers

### storage
- **Why:** To save your usage statistics
- **What we do:** Store count and last-sent info locally
- **What we don't do:** Sync this data or send it anywhere

### host_permissions (notebooklm.google.com)
- **Why:** To show the helpful copy button in NotebookLM
- **What we do:** Display a UI element on NotebookLM pages
- **What we don't do:** Access your notebooks or Google account
